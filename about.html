Founder profile

I am a passionate graphic designer with nearly four years of experience specializing in brand identity, textile design, and speculative design. I focus on creating visually impactful and inclusive designs that solve real-world problems.

I built VisionX to address the challenges faced by color-blind individuals in design and digital interfaces — aiming to make visual content more accessible and inclusive for everyone.
